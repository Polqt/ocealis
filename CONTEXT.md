# Ocealis

An anonymous open-ocean message-in-a-bottle world: visitors cast bottles from shore, browse seas on a map, open corks, stamp journeys, and re-release bottles into the unknown.

## Language

### World objects

**Bottle**:
A message sealed in a cork that drifts on the ocean as a long-lived world object.
_Avoid_: Post, letter, mail, email, tweet

**Cork**:
The map-visible marker for a Bottle once it is visible in the ocean.
_Avoid_: Pin, marker, marker icon (when speaking in product language)

**Message**:
The written body inside a Bottle, authored at cast time and not rewritten later.
_Avoid_: Content, caption, post body

**Nickname**:
A required display name attached to a cast or re-release; not an account.
_Avoid_: User, username, account, profile

**Seed Bottle**:
A system- or creator-authored Bottle placed so the ocean is never empty for first visitors.
_Avoid_: Fake user, bot message, demo post

### Places and motion

**Ocean**:
The only valid place to browse or drop Bottles; land is inert.
_Avoid_: Map (prefer Ocean when meaning the water), sea region (unless naming a basin)

**Shoreline**:
The coast line used to snap an inland cast to a just-offshore drop point.
_Avoid_: Beach (unless UI copy), coast point

**Drift**:
The ongoing movement of a Bottle across the Ocean over time.
_Avoid_: Animation-only motion, teleport

**Sink**:
The end of a Bottle's life after a long stay in the Ocean (years), when it leaves the world.
_Avoid_: Delete, expire (prefer Sink for product language), soft-delete

### Discovery and interaction

**Cast**:
Releasing a new Bottle into the Ocean from the visitor's nearest Shoreline.
_Avoid_: Post, publish, send, submit

**Open**:
Reading a Bottle's Message without claiming ownership.
_Avoid_: Claim, catch, collect, unlock

**Stamp**:
A passport-style mark on a Bottle's Journey: seal icon and/or short note.
_Avoid_: Like, reaction, comment, review

**Re-release**:
Casting an existing Bottle again from the finder's Shoreline so it continues elsewhere.
_Avoid_: Repost, share, forward

**Journey**:
The ordered history of events for one Bottle (cast, drift, stamp, re-release, sink).
_Avoid_: Timeline, log, audit trail (in product copy)

**Mystery Delay**:
The period after Cast or Re-release when a Bottle is invisible on the map before it joins the Ocean as a Cork.
_Avoid_: Cooldown, pending queue (in product copy)

### People

**Visitor**:
Anyone using Ocealis without an account.
_Avoid_: User, member, sailor (unless flavor copy), anonymous user
